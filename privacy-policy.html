Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

At TiChia, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your data with complete transparency. We believe you should have full control over your personal information.

Section 01

Data Collection

TiChia accesses your location data (GPS coordinates) solely to provide the core functionality of logging and mapping your work activities. We only collect what's necessary to deliver the service you expect.

Section 02

Data Storage

All your location logs, notes, and photos are stored entirely on your device. TiChia has no servers—we never receive, store, or have access to your data. It physically never leaves your device.

Section 03

Information Sharing

Since your data never reaches us, there is nothing to sell or share. We cannot access, trade, or transfer your information—because we simply don't have it.

Section 04

User Control

You can manage or delete your location data within the app at any time. We provide you with complete control over your information—no hidden data, no complications.

Our Commitment to You

Privacy isn't just a policy—it's a promise. We built TiChia with privacy-first principles because we believe your data belongs to you.

We're happy to explain anything about our privacy practices.
